Upload a PDF or image file (JPG, PNG, etc.) of your Costco receipt. You can also take a photo with your phone camera.

Instructions:

  1. Take a photo: Use your phone camera to snap a clear photo of your receipt.
  2. Or upload a file: Scan or download your receipt as a PDF or image.
  3. Make sure the text is clear and readable (don't worry about strikethrough marks).
  4. Click "Choose File" to select your receipt.
  5. Click "Upload & Process" to analyze your receipt.
Photo Tips:
  • Take photos in good lighting with minimal shadows
  • Keep the receipt flat and avoid creases when possible
  • The system can read through Costco's checkout strikethrough marks
  • Photo uploads may require manual review for accuracy

Supported formats: PDF, JPG, PNG, WebP, AVIF, GIF, BMP
The system works best with clear, well-lit images and text-based PDFs.
